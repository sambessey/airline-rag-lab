# IRROPS Fee Waivers and Fare Rules

**Policy ID:** REV-IRROPS-020  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective:** 2026-01-01  
**Region:** Global  
**Owner:** Revenue Management

## Change Fee Waiver
When IRROPS is confirmed:
- **Change fees are waived** for all customers for rebooking to travel within **7 days**.
- **Fare difference** may be waived if rebooked to the same cabin and within **24 hours** of original departure.

## Cabin Downgrade
If only a lower cabin is available:
- Offer downgrade only with explicit consent.
- Provide a refund of the cabin difference according to standard refund rules.

## Same-Day Priority
Same-day reaccommodation should be attempted before offering:
- voluntary cancellation
- travel credits
- vouchers

## Exclusions
- Discretionary upgrades are not permitted as a substitute for reaccommodation.
- “Name change” is not permitted under IRROPS waiver.
