# Partner Airline Rebooking Guidelines

**Policy ID:** OPS-PARTNER-030  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective:** 2026-01-01  
**Region:** Global  
**Owner:** Alliances & Partnerships

## When Partner Rebooking is Allowed
Partner rebooking is allowed when:
- No same-day itinerary is available on our own network, AND
- The partner itinerary arrives within **12 hours** of the earliest alternative.

## Eligibility Checks (Must Pass)
Before confirming a partner option:
- Ticket stock supports interline/partner issuance
- Fare rules allow involuntary reroute
- Destination and routing are permitted for the ticket type
