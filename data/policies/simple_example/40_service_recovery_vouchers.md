# Service Recovery: Vouchers, Meals, Hotel

**Policy ID:** CX-SVC-040  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective:** 2026-01-01  
**Region:** Global  
**Owner:** Customer Experience

## Meal Voucher
If delay is **180+ minutes** and customer is at the airport:
- Issue meal voucher up to **$25** per passenger.

## Hotel Guidance (Overnight Delay)
If reaccommodation requires overnight travel due to IRROPS:
- Provide hotel guidance when available.
- If customer arranges their own hotel, reimbursement is considered up to a regional cap.

## Travel Credit vs Refund
- If customer chooses not to travel due to IRROPS, offer:
  - refund to original form of payment OR
  - travel credit (valid 12 months)
- Present both options neutrally.
