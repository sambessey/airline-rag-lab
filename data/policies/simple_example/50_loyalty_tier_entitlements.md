# Loyalty Tier Entitlements During Disruption

**Policy ID:** LOYALTY-050  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective:** 2026-01-01  
**Region:** Global  
**Owner:** Loyalty

## Tiers
- **Silver:** standard service
- **Gold:** priority reaccommodation queue
- **Platinum:** priority reaccommodation + proactive outreach when possible

## Gold Tier: Reaccommodation Priority
For Gold customers during IRROPS:
- Attempt same-day reaccommodation first.
- If multiple options exist, choose the one with the earliest arrival time.
- Offer partner rebooking when eligible (see Partner Guidelines).

## Platinum Tier: Additional Handling
- If customer is stranded overnight, proactively offer hotel guidance and meal voucher where applicable.
- Proactively communicate rebooking plan when possible.
